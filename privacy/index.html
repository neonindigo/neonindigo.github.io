Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2026

1. Who We Are

NeonIndigo is a trading name of Michael Robinson. We develop apps for Apple platforms. If you have any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@neonindigo.com.

2. What This Policy Covers

This policy explains how we handle information when you use any NeonIndigo app or contact us for support. It does not apply to third-party services or websites we do not operate.

3. Information We Collect

Anonymous usage analytics. Our apps collect analytics data to help us understand how they are used and to improve them. This data includes:

  • App usage patterns (features used, frequency of use)
  • Device category (e.g. iPhone, iPad, Mac)
  • App version and operating system version

This data is collected in a form that cannot reasonably be used to identify you. It is not linked to your name, Apple ID, or any other personal identifier. Because this data is truly anonymous, the UK GDPR does not apply to it.

Server and infrastructure logs. Our analytics infrastructure may automatically generate logs that include IP addresses, request timestamps, and similar technical data. These logs are used solely for security and operational purposes.

Support correspondence. If you contact us by email, we will receive your email address and any personal information you include in your message.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (UK GDPR)

  • Anonymous analytics: Not personal data; UK GDPR does not apply.
  • Server logs (including IP addresses): Legitimate interests — ensuring the security and operational integrity of our systems.
  • Support emails: Legitimate interests — providing support to users who contact us.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To improve our apps and services
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To maintain the security of our systems

6. Data Retention

  • Anonymous analytics: Retained indefinitely in aggregated form.
  • Server logs: Retained for up to 30 days.
  • Support correspondence: Retained for as long as necessary to resolve your query, and no longer than 2 years thereafter.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share personal data with third parties, except where required to do so by law or to protect our legal rights.

8. Your Rights

Where we process personal data about you (for example, via server logs or support emails), you have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request rectification or erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to our processing of your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@neonindigo.com. We will respond within one month.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version. Continued use of our apps after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@neonindigo.com.
